## Mr. May D and Wande Coal comes through with a sweet joint dubbed “Call My Name”.

Talented singer/pop vocalist, **May D** teamed up with Black Diamond vocal pop master – **Wande Coal** and delivered “**Call My Name**“. This new record here is the first track on MrMayD’s new music project **“May Day”** _(_[_High With You_](https://audiomack.com/album/may-d-1/may-day)_)_ The EP.

The new body of work (EP) released on 2020’s May Day (May 1st) consist of 6 tracks all produced by Vstix (Alfred Victor Ayokanmi) except the 6th track (produced by Dante). With 3 special features; Wande Coal, 9ice and Stanley Enow.

“Call My Name” is a complete track with good vibes/melody which sees Wande going back to his Mushin2Mo’Hit days. I promise you would love this…. Listen and Enjoy!
