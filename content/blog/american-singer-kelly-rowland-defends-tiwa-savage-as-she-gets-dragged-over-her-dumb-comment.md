## **American singer, Kelly Rowland immediately came to Tiwa Savage’s rescue after she was dragged for making a dumb comment.**

**Kelly Rowland** broke the internet when she posted a photo of her enviable physique in just a bra and panties. The 39-year-old mother of one captioned the photo; _“It’s not a thirst trap…I’m just gonna leave this here. #39”_ and her fans took to her comment section to drool over her never aging body and **Tiwa Savage** wasn’t left out.

The singer who is also a mother of little Jamil left a comment hailing the singer for not having any stretch marks._“omg, not even a single stretch mark”_ However, some Nigerians felt her comment was dumb and a big disrespect to women. A fan slammed Tiwa for making women feel insecure about their bodies with her comment. _“That’s a dumb compliment considering she has them. Its talk like this that makes women feel insecure about with bodies. She has the, & she looks beautiful. That’s all you needed to say…”_

**Kelly** noticing the drama came to ease the already building tension by revealing that she actually has stretch marks and would post a picture showing them very soon. _“I have em! Will post a picture sooner or later so you can see. I ain’t got nothing to hide.”_
